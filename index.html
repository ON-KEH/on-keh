About ON

A premium residential partner for life in Korea.

ON is built for clients who need more than a place to stay. We connect housing, settlement, and living support into one coordinated experience.

WarmthThoughtful support that helps clients feel welcomed and settled.
HospitalityA service mindset that extends beyond the housing itself.
ReliabilityCorporate-level coordination, clear communication, and practical execution.
TailoringHousing and services matched to budget, duration, family needs, and lifestyle.

ON Korea Executive Housing supports short-, mid-, and long-term stays in Korea through customized housing and operational support. Depending on each client's needs, we coordinate rental homes, serviced residences, furnishing, utility setup, cost planning, and related administrative arrangements.

Our main focus is corporate housing for companies with expatriate employees, assignees, and international hires. We also support individual residents seeking a reliable stay in Korea and property owners who wish to offer suitable homes to qualified clients.

"A home that meets global standards, in the heart of Korea."
